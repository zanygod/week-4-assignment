Best Momo's in the World

Welcome to Nepali Khaja Ghar, a cozy culinary haven nestled in South Korea. Founded and managed by Nepali students, our restaurant offers a delightful journey through the flavors of Nepal. From aromatic curries to savory momos, chatpate, chowmin and crispy pakodas, we bring you an authentic taste of Nepali cuisine. Indulge in traditional snacks like sel roti and Yomari, transporting you to the bustling streets of Kathmandu. Join us at "Nepali Khaja Ghar" for a gastronomic adventure like no other.
